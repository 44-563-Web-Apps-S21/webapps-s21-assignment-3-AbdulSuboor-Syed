Red Monkey

Dr.Raymond Higgs was a famous bio engineer who was once regarded as a prodigy

During an experiment with a rare monkey genes an explosion occured .

After years of being in the hospital the doctor discovered that during the experiment the genes of monkey mixed with Dr Higgs and gave him super human strength but it affected his pyschological behaviour.

He blames Dr.James for his failure in the experiment as it was Dr.James company that supplied him with the testing equipment

Dr Higgs is also on the hunt to find Dr.James but on a mission to kill Dr.James and now he calls himself The Red Monkey for his skin has turned red his body and mind of a monkey .

  1. Go to the North West side of the Amazonian Forest
  2. Follow along the river till you reach a hugh oak tree
  3. Under the tree there is a cave
  4. Follow the path into the cave and you will reach a cavern
  5. There you will find the lair of Dr Higgs along with a large group of Monkeys whom he has command over
